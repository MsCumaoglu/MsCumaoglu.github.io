Filora PDF – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Filora PDF ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Filora PDF does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

We do not require account creation, and we do not collect:

* Name, email address, or contact information
* Location data
* Personal files or documents

2. File Access and Usage

Filora PDF allows users to:

* Select PDF or image files from their device
* Create, edit, merge, or convert PDF files

All file operations are performed locally on the user’s device.

We do not:

* Upload files to any server
* Store files externally
* Share files with third parties

Users explicitly choose files using the system file picker (Storage Access Framework), ensuring full control over file access.

3. Storage Access

Filora PDF uses privacy-friendly storage access methods in compliance with Android policies.

* The app does NOT request "All Files Access"
* Files are accessed only when the user selects them
* Files may be saved either:

  * In app-specific storage
  * In a location chosen by the user

4. Camera Usage

The app may request camera permission to scan documents and create PDFs.

* Images captured are processed locally
* No camera data is transmitted or stored externally

5. Advertising

Filora PDF may use Google AdMob to display ads.

AdMob may collect:

* Device identifiers
* Usage data for personalized or non-personalized ads

For more information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

All operations are performed locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data externally.

7. Third-Party Services

Filora PDF uses the following third-party services:

* Google AdMob (advertising)

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Filora PDF does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our official page.
