# Privacy Policy for MuteMaster

**Last updated:** May 12, 2026

Welcome to MuteMaster.

MuteMaster is a focus and notification management application designed to help users reduce distractions by controlling and filtering notifications during scheduled focus sessions.

This Privacy Policy explains what data MuteMaster accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

---

# 1. Information We Access

MuteMaster may access the following information on your device:

## Notification Data
When Notification Access permission is granted, MuteMaster can access:

- Notification app name
- Notification title
- Notification message text
- Notification receive time

This access is required for the app’s core functionality:
- Blocking distracting notifications
- Allowing selected apps during focus sessions
- Displaying blocked notification history

## Installed Applications
MuteMaster reads the list of installed applications on your device so you can:
- Select allowed apps
- Create app groups
- Configure focus plans

## Advertising Identifier (Ad ID)
If ads are enabled, Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to provide and improve advertising services.

---

# 2. How Your Data Is Used

MuteMaster uses accessed information only for app functionality, including:

- Managing focus sessions
- Filtering notifications
- Allowing selected apps
- Displaying blocked notification history
- Scheduling focus plans

MuteMaster does NOT:
- Upload your notification content to external servers
- Sell your personal data
- Share your notification content with third parties
- Use your notifications for profiling or tracking

---

# 3. Local Device Storage

Notification history and app settings are stored locally on your device.

MuteMaster does not operate its own cloud servers for notification storage.

You can clear stored notification history at any time from the app settings.

Uninstalling the app removes locally stored application data according to your device settings.

---

# 4. Advertising

MuteMaster uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and process certain data, including:
- Advertising ID
- Device information
- Ad interaction data

Advertising may be personalized depending on your consent choices and region.

Users in supported regions will be shown a consent form before personalized ads are displayed.

You can change your ad preferences at any time inside:
Settings → Privacy & Legal → Manage Ad Preferences

For more information about Google's advertising policies:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

# 5. Permissions Used

MuteMaster may request the following permissions:

## Notification Access
Required to:
- Detect incoming notifications
- Block distracting notifications
- Allow selected apps
- Maintain blocked notification history

## Exact Alarm Permission
Required to:
- Activate and deactivate focus sessions at precise scheduled times

## Boot Permission
Required to:
- Restore focus schedules after device restart

---

# 6. Data Sharing

MuteMaster does not share notification content or blocked notification history with third parties.

Advertising-related data may be processed by Google AdMob according to Google's policies.

---

# 7. Data Security

MuteMaster is designed to keep your notification data on your device whenever possible.

However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

# 8. Children's Privacy

MuteMaster is not directed toward children under the age required by local digital consent laws.
